About Us

Our story, philosophy, and the team behind MIRAI STUDIO

Our Story

Founded in 2018, MIRAI STUDIO was born from a vision to create a sanctuary where hair artistry and client experience are elevated to new heights. The name "Mirai" means "future" in Japanese, reflecting our forward-thinking approach and dedication to innovation.

Nestled in the vibrant Nedre Løkka/Sofienberg district, our salon combines minimalist Scandinavian design with Japanese-inspired precision and attention to detail.

Our Philosophy

The guiding principles behind everything we do

Artistry

We approach hair as an art form, blending technical expertise with creative vision to craft styles that enhance each client's unique beauty.

Sustainability

Our commitment to the environment is reflected in our eco-conscious product choices, minimal waste practices, and energy-efficient salon design.

Education

We believe in continuous learning, ensuring our team stays at the cutting edge of global hair trends, techniques, and innovations.

Experience

Every visit to MIRAI STUDIO is crafted to be more than just a haircut—it's a rejuvenating experience for mind and body.
